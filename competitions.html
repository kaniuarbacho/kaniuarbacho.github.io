Let me also mention the following:

  • ‘BWM 2016 Round 2 Solution 2’ is one of my most favorite proofs I have seen so far. By assuming the finiteness of certain integers (related to primes and triangular numbers), we show that 1 = 0 in 𝔽2. Wow!
  • ‘BWM 2017 Round 2 Exercise 4’ was the most challenging problem I have worked on so far. I have been trying to solve this problem from morning till night for two weeks without taking any break. Then I finally solved it. :-)
